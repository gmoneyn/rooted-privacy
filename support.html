Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a plant?

Tap the + button on the My Garden screen. You can search the built-in library of 500+ plants or create a custom entry with your own care schedule.

How do care reminders work?

Rooted schedules local notifications based on each plant's care schedule. Make sure notifications are enabled in your iPhone's Settings > Rooted > Notifications.

How do I remove ads?

Go to Settings within the app and tap "Remove Ads." It's a one-time purchase of $2.99 that permanently removes all advertising.

Where is my data stored?

All your data stays on your device. We don't have any servers. If you delete the app, your data is removed. We recommend backing up your iPhone regularly via iCloud or your computer.

The weather isn't loading

Go to Settings in the app and check your location setting. You can either enable "Use My Location" (requires location permission) or search for your city manually.

I purchased Remove Ads but still see ads

Try restarting the app. If the issue persists, go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases." If that doesn't work, contact us below.
